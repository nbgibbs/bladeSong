Ranged attacks are made by first rolling to hit (base of 3 plus agility plus weapon proficiency) then rolling for damage (weapon specific).

The dificulty to hit will be based on range and size of target.

Bows will have a strength requierment and if you don't meet the requierment a to hit penalty will be imposed.


# Examples:
hunting bow 
strength requierment: 0
damage: 3d
